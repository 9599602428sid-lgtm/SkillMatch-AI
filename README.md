# 🎯 SkillMatch Path

**SkillMatch Path** is an AI-powered career guidance web app that matches your skills to the most relevant job roles — helping you discover where you fit best in today's job market.

🔗 **Live App:** [skill-match-path.base44.app](https://skill-match-path.base44.app)

---

## 💡 What It Does

Users can input their current skills and the app intelligently maps them to matching job roles, giving a clearer picture of which career paths they're best suited for.

Whether you're a fresher figuring out your direction or a professional exploring a pivot, SkillMatch Path cuts through the confusion and points you toward roles that align with what you already know.

---

## ✨ Features

- 🔍 **Skill-to-Role Matching** — Enter your skills and get matched to relevant job roles instantly
- ⚡ **AI-Powered Analysis** — Uses AI to intelligently evaluate skill relevance across roles
- 🌐 **Web-Based** — No installation needed, runs entirely in the browser
